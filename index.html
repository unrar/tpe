jCurrency

jCurrency is the first product of TwentyPeople Europe to be distributed under TPI. It's a very simple but expansible currency convertor, written in Java. Due to our CompatibiliTeam work, it's compatible with all the platforms with JRE >= 6!

AGE

AGE isn't distributed by TwentyPeople International, but for our nice developer unrar himself. It's a framework for Javascript (using jQuery sometimes) that, at the moment, allows you to very easily add an age verification system to your page. Its best feature is the use of the new technology show&hide, that removes the need of reloading pages, without using Ajax!.
